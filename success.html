Have you ever wondered what might be under your land?

Get to know what is underneath your land by just scanning your land with diggit on your phone to know which natural resources is present beneath.












App Features







Scan Land

Scan your land by taking live video of your land or taking existing pictures from your gallery.





Detect Resources

After thorough scanning the app notifies you if any natural resources is present underneath or not.





Get Buyer

The app connects you with the right buyers so you know the best person to sell to.







Get To Know How The App Works





Diggit helps you to uncover the mystery of hidden natural resources that may be present underneath your land by scanning the land through your phone camera to discover if mineral resources is present. This our technology uses the power of Artificial Intelligence to fully predict if mineral resources is present or not and the type of mineral resources present. It also connects you with the right buyer after the resources has been discovered.
